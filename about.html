About Project Butterfly 🦋

Project Butterfly is a place of hope, support and encouragement for NICU families and parents of premature babies.

Created from a journey of love and resilience, our mission is to remind parents that their tiny fighters are strong and their stories matter.

We believe every milestone deserves to be celebrated — from the smallest breath to the biggest achievements.

Our Story 💛

Project Butterfly was born from a journey filled with courage, hope, and love. Inspired by the experience of caring for a premature baby, our mission is to walk beside NICU families, offering encouragement, practical resources, and a community that understands.

We believe that every tiny milestone deserves to be celebrated and that no family should feel alone during their NICU journey. Together, we help every little butterfly spread its wings.

Our Mission 🦋

To provide hope, trusted information, and compassionate support for NICU families while celebrating the incredible strength of premature babies and the people who love them.

Our Vision 🌍

A world where every NICU family feels supported, informed, and empowered from the first day of their journey through every milestone that follows.

Meet Our Founder 🌸

Hello! I'm Ernestina, the founder of Project Butterfly. My passion for supporting NICU families comes from personal experience and a deep desire to ensure that no parent feels alone during one of life's most challenging journeys.

Project Butterfly was created with love, hope, and the belief that even the smallest butterflies can grow strong wings. Together, we celebrate every milestone and remind families that hope is always within reach.
