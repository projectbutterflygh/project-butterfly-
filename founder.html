Meet the Founder 🦋

Turning one family's journey into hope for many others.

Hello, I'm Ernestina.

Project Butterfly was inspired by my daughter Annabelle's journey through the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU). During that season, I experienced fear, hope, uncertainty, and overwhelming love.

I created Project Butterfly so that other families would know they are not alone. My dream is to provide encouragement, trusted resources, and practical support for parents walking a similar path.

Every baby deserves hope. Every family deserves support.

Our Promise 💛

We promise to continue growing Project Butterfly with compassion, honesty, and care—always putting families first and celebrating every tiny milestone.
