💛 Caring for Yourself

While your baby is receiving care, remember that you matter too. Rest when you can, accept help from loved ones, and be kind to yourself. You do not have to carry this journey alone.

🏥 Life in the NICU

The NICU can feel overwhelming at first. It is okay to ask questions, learn about your baby's care, and take each day one step at a time.

🏡 Preparing to Go Home

Going home is an exciting milestone. Before discharge, make sure you understand your baby's medications, feeding plan, follow-up appointments, and emergency contacts.

🌈 Celebrate Every Milestone

Every gram gained, every successful feeding, every smile, and every new achievement deserves to be celebrated. Small victories become amazing stories.

🦋 Remember This

You are stronger than you know. Your baby is stronger than you imagine. Hope grows one day at a time.
