Annabelle's Story 🦋

The journey that inspired Project Butterfly.

Where Hope Began 💛

Annabelle's early arrival introduced our family to the world of the NICU—a place filled with uncertainty, courage, and incredible strength. Every small milestone became a reason to celebrate, and every day taught us the power of hope.

Her journey inspired the creation of Project Butterfly so that other NICU families would know they are not alone.

Our Promise 🌸

Through Project Butterfly, we hope to encourage parents, share reliable resources, and celebrate every tiny victory. Every butterfly deserves the chance to spread its wings.
